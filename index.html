I am a student and studying
finance and management.
The goal is to reach
somewhere very few belong
to, although is a very task.
But I have decided to work
harder to achieve everything
I want to have in future. 
An introverted person who
lives in dreams although life
is more practical. Always
think practically because
there is no place for emotion
to me.
Never go for any argument,
try to be calm, can not react
immediately, love
programming. generate new
ideas, learn things deeply.
Quantum physic is the love
of my life and commerce is
the present wife who is
being cheated by me.
Enjoy asking questions
rather than answering the
question. However, you are
totally ok with me whether
you are a shy or a talkative
person.
